Mechanics Scroll Mastery Skill Level = clamp(Round(Mastery% ÷ 5), 1, 20) Resist Chance clamp( target.magicResist × 0.0005 + clamp((target.level − caster.level) × 0.005, −0.1, 0.1) − caster.accuracy , 0, 0.9) Dispel Mechanics You cast this on a monster to remove its beneficial buffs. Whether it removes anything, and how much, is decided in two steps. 1. Landing. The monster first rolls to resist (the Resist Chance above), which your Accuracy lowers. If the monster resists, nothing is removed. 2. Removal, only when it lands. Each of the monster's buffs is removed only when a random value from 0 to 1 is greater than that buff's Dispel Resist after subtracting your Dispel Resist reduction. A buff with 0 Dispel Resist is always removed. Dispel Resist reduction = clamp(round(Mastery% ÷ 5), 1, 20) × 0.01 Your reduction grows with your Scroll Mastery rank, lowering a monster's Dispel Resist by 1 percentage point per effective rank (up to 20). So a scroll dispel uses two of your stats: Accuracy to land it in step 1, and Scroll Mastery to strip more buffs in step 2.
