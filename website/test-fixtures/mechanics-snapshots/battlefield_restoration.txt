Mechanics Healing Formula Cleric (player), Druid (player), Rogue (player), Warrior (player), Wizard (player) Final Heal = Base Heal + round(Base Heal × min(WIS × 0.004, 5.0)) Ranger (player) Final Heal = Base Heal + round(Base Heal × min(WIS × 3 × 0.004, 5.0)) Ranger wisdom triples the heal bonus (effective cap: WIS = 417). Effective Cast Time = castTime − (castTime × spellHaste)
