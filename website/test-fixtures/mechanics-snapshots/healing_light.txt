Mechanics When used by a mercenary, skill level = floor(regular level ÷ 5) + floor(veteran level ÷ 10), capped at max skill level. Healing Formula Final Heal = Base Heal + RoundToInt(Base Heal × min(WIS × 0.004, 5.0)) Critical Heal: on crit → 90% ×2.0 | 10% ×3.0 Effective Cast Time = castTime − (castTime × spellHaste)
