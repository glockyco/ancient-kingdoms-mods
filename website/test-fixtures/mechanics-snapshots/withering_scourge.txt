Mechanics Resist Chance clamp( target.diseaseResist × 0.0005 + clamp((target.level − caster.level) × 0.005, −0.1, 0.1) − caster.accuracy , 0, 0.9) Dispel Mechanics Monsters use this to remove beneficial buffs from you and your pets. Whether it removes anything is decided in two steps. 1. Landing. You first roll to resist it (the Resist Chance above). The monster's Accuracy lowers your resist chance, while a higher matching resistance raises it. If you resist, nothing is removed. 2. Removal, only when it lands. All of your buffs are removed except the Rest buff. On a pet, all of its buffs are removed. Effective Cast Time = castTime − (castTime × spellHaste)
